Privacy Policy

At Pepeha Generator, protecting your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We are fully committed to ensuring that your personal data is handled with care, and any information collected through your interaction with our platform will be treated with confidentiality. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

Information We Collect

The Pepeha Generator only collects information that you voluntarily provide, such as your name, home location, family names, and other details you include when completing your Pepeha. None of this information is stored on our servers, and we do not collect information without your consent.

How We Use Your Information

  • Your Pepeha data is generated solely based on the provided inputs, and is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • We do not share, sell, or distribute any of your personal data to third parties for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.
  • All information input into the Pepeha Generator is processed internally and is used only for session-based generation purposes.

Data Security

We have implemented robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse of your information. All information is processed within the system's internal mechanisms and is not exposed to third parties. Our security efforts aim to protect the privacy and confidentiality of your inputs.

Data Retention

We do not retain your data. Once the Pepeha is generated, the inputs are discarded immediately, ensuring that your information is not stored on our servers beyond the immediate generation session.

AI Usage and Data Handling

This website utilises artificial intelligence (AI) to accurately translate from English place names to Maori place names, as well as generate personalized Pepehas. However, we want to assure you that:

  • The data used to train the AI model is not derived from your interactions with this website.
  • Each time you generate a Pepeha, the AI processes your input in real-time and discards the data immediately after generating the result.
  • None of your personal information is retained or used to improve the AI model.
  • By using this service, you consent to the temporary processing of your input data for the sole purpose of generating your Pepeha.

API Requests and Security

For secure API requests, we utilize Groq's services. Their privacy policy and terms of service apply to all data transmitted through their platform. You can find more information at:

  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • Groq Terms of Use

Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to access the information you provide during the session. Since no data is stored, you are in full control of the inputs you provide, and nothing is permanently associated with your usage of our generator.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time, and any amendments will be reflected on this page. We encourage our users to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us using the Contact Form
