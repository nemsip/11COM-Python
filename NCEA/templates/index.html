About the Pepeha Generator

The Pepeha Generator helps you create a pepeha that can be used in introductions, presentations, and cultural settings. A pepeha is a traditional Māori way of introducing oneself and connecting with one's heritage.

By filling out a simple form, you can generate a pepeha that includes your name, family, home, and other personal details. It's a meaningful way to share your identity and cultural background.
